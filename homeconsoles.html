Introduction

When video games were created the did not have much of a wide following. they were developed and used by scientists in Universities around the world. It was not until video game companies began to develop their own games to be bought and played. While the introduction of new video games helps expand the industry a decent amount, it wasn't until home video game consoles began being developed and sold to the public that the video game industry started to grow in popularity and wealth. The introduction of home console gaming got rid of the market for arcade gaming. Home console gaming allowed for people to stay in the comfort of their own homes while at the same time being able to enjoy the video games they loved so much and it was for these reasons that home console gaming took over the video game industry.

The Progression to Home Console Gaming

As video game companies began to develop their own home consoles and games for customers to buy, certain video game consoles gained more popular than others. A multitude of companies attempted to create successful consoles however, these consoles did not gather enough popularity to remain financially successful. As time went on within the video gaming industry, new and more technologically advanced versions of old consoles were released for the public to enjoy. As new and more technologically advanced consoles were being released, more recent and advanced games were also released alongside the consoles. This allowed companies to have a steady stream of customers wanting to buy the new and usually more expensive console to play the new games. Home Console gaming had truly taken over the video game industry. Modern Home Console Gaming

Over time, the technology behind video gaming consoles has improved. In earlier consoles, games were made of simple designs and only 2d worlds. While this was an amazing technological advancement for that time period, in today's current video game standards those games would not hold up. Current video game consoles are able to render 3d visuals and depict the worlds and art created by video game designers in high quality. The video games crafted by video game designers are built up of entire virtual worlds that the customer is able to explore to their heart's content. Modern gaming consoles such as the Playstation 4, the Xbox One, and the Nintendo Switch show just how far video game technology had come.
